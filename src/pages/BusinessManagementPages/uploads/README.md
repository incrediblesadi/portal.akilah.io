# Upload folder for business management assets
This folder contains uploaded assets for business management including:
- Business logos
- Branding materials
- Documents
- Certificates
- Images

## File Structure
- `/logos/` - Business logos and branding images
- `/documents/` - Business documents and certificates
- `/images/` - General business images
- `/temp/` - Temporary upload files

## Upload Guidelines
- Maximum file size: 10MB
- Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, SVG, PDF
- Files are processed and optimized automatically
- Temporary files are cleaned up after 24 hours
